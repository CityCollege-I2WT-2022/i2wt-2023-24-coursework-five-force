American Advancements in World War II

The strongest weapons to come out of WW2 the Little boy and the Fat Man.

The creation of the nuclear bomb took a lot of time and it was a race between the germans and the americans to see who first can create it. The first atomic bomb, named Little Boy, was dropped on Hiroshima from the Enola Gay, a B-29 bomber, at 8:15 AM on August 6, 1945. The second bomb, named Fat Man, was dropped on Nagasaki from the Bockscar, also a B-29 bomber, at 11:02 AM on August 9, 1945.
